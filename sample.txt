Apache Solr is a highly reliable, scalable and fault tolerant, providing distributed indexing, replication and load-balanced querying, automated failover and recovery, centralized configuration and more. Solr powers the search and navigation features of many of the world's largest internet sites.

The unified highlighter in Lucene and Solr provides advanced text highlighting capabilities with better performance and more features than previous highlighting implementations. It can highlight text with HTML tags, showing users exactly where their search terms appear in documents.
